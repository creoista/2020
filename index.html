ABOUT US

CREOISTA 2.0 is the Second National Level ROBOFEST conducted by department of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering of Sri Sairam Engineering College. After the inauguration and grand success of CREOISTA’19, the legacy continues, this time in a completely virtual mode. Thriving over 8 different events Creoista2.0 provides a platform for the young tech fanatics and masterminds to overcome the odds and win exciting cash awards both in technical as well as non-technical aspects. With “ROBOTICS & AUTOMATION” as its theme, CREOISTA 2.0 is all set to cheerfully welcome the geeky enthusiasts in you.
