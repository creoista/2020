    DEBATRONZ

    We present you the Evolutionary debate contest. If you want to express your thoughts, Grab this opportunity and know what others think on the same topic.

    DEBATRONZ

    rounds

      Round 1 :
    • (GRAB THE TIME) Contestants will analyse and express their views on the given topic in the stipulated time assigned to them. Shortlisted candidates from this round will move to round 2.

      • Round 2:
    • (SELF-OBSTACLE) Contestants will talk about the pros and cons of the topics assigned to them. Clear expression of ideas will make the contestants move to round 3.

      • Round 3 :
    • (DARE TO SPEAK) This is one on one round. As one contestant speak the pros of a topic given, randomly chosen next contestant will speak the cons of the same topic. The contestant with more points will be declared as the winner.
